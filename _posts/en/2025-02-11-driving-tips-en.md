- Inflate tires twice a year.

- Car repairers in small towns may be experts.

- Drive until it becomes second nature.

- In a parking lot, you can use the empty parking spaces to help you park.

- Pay attention to rods that are lower than the front of your car. You might not see them and hit them.

- Drive at the same speed as the cars in your surroundings.

- Buy an extra pair of small rearview mirrors to stick to the main mirror, and angle them differently so you can see more.
