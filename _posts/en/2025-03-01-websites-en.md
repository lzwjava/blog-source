Here is a curated list of useful websites for various purposes:

- **IP Address Information**: [Show My IP](https://www.showmyip.com) - A tool to check your public IP address.

- **JSON Web Tokens**: [JWT.io](https://jwt.io) - A website for encoding and decoding JSON Web Tokens.

- **Base64 Encoding/Decoding**: [Base64 Encode](https://www.base64encode.org) - A tool for encoding and decoding data in Base64 format.

- **Internet Speed Test**: [Speedtest.net](https://www.speedtest.net) - A service to test your internet connection speed.

- **Website Traffic Analysis**: [SimilarWeb](https://www.similarweb.com) - Provides insights into website traffic and analytics.

- **Trending Topics**: [Google Trends](https://trends.google.com/trends/) - Explore what the world is searching for.

- **Web Security and Performance**: [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com) - Offers a range of services to enhance website security and performance.

- **Domain Registration**: [Namecheap](https://www.namecheap.com) - A platform for purchasing and managing domain names.
