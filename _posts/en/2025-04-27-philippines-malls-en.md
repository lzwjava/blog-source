The Philippines is renowned for its large and numerous shopping malls, which serve as major retail, dining, and entertainment destinations. Several stand out for their immense size and popularity:

Located in Metro Manila, **SM City North EDSA** in Quezon City is frequently cited as the largest mall in the Philippines and one of the biggest in the world by retail floor area. It's a sprawling complex with a vast array of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options.

Another colossal mall in Metro Manila is **SM Megamall** in Mandaluyong. It is consistently ranked among the largest in the country and offers a comprehensive shopping experience with diverse retail outlets, dining establishments, and recreational facilities.

**SM Mall of Asia (SM MOA)** in Pasay, situated along the Manila Bay, is another iconic and massive mall. Known for its impressive size and bayside location, it features a wide selection of stores, restaurants, an IMAX theater, and the SMX Convention Center.

Outside of Metro Manila, **SM Seaside City Cebu** in Cebu City is a prominent and large shopping mall in the Visayas region, contributing significantly to Cebu's retail landscape with its extensive facilities and offerings.

**Gaisano Mall of Davao** and **SM City Davao** are among the major shopping destinations in Davao City, Mindanao, catering to the needs of the region's growing population with a wide variety of retail and lifestyle options.
