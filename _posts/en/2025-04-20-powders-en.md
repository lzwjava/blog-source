2. **Beer Powder**: This is a dehydrated version of beer and can be used in various recipes to add a malty flavor. It is made by spray drying concentrated beer with maltodextrin, resulting in an easy-to-use powder.

3. **Espresso Powder**: This is finely ground, dehydrated coffee beans. It is often used to add depth to chocolate-based desserts and baked goods, but it can also be used in savory recipes.
