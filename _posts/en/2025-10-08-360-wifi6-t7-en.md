### 360 Wi-Fi 6 Router T7 Product Introduction

The 360 Wi-Fi 6 Router T7 (model: 360T7) is an entry-level home wireless router developed by Qihoo 360 Technology Co., Ltd., a Chinese cybersecurity and smart hardware company. Launched around September 2022, it's designed primarily for users with gigabit fiber broadband connections. This AX3000-class dual-band router supports the latest Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) standard, offering stable and high-speed wireless coverage suitable for small to medium-sized households (up to about 100-150 square meters). It's commonly bundled as a complimentary device or standard equipment in telecom operator packages from providers like China Telecom, China Unicom, and China Mobile, with variants such as T7U and T7M for specific carriers.

#### Key Features
- **Wi-Fi 6 Technology**: Delivers faster speeds, better efficiency in crowded networks, and improved range compared to Wi-Fi 5. Supports OFDMA and MU-MIMO for handling multiple devices simultaneously without lag.
- **Easy Setup and Management**: App-based configuration via the 360 Smart Home app, with features like parental controls, guest networks, and security scans powered by 360's antivirus expertise.
- **Operator Customization**: Tailored for seamless integration with Chinese ISPs, including easy PPPoE dialing and IPv6 support.
- **Compact Design**: Sleek, wall-mountable form factor with an LED indicator for status. It's energy-efficient and runs quietly.
