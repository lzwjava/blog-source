### What is Haikang Vision (Hikvision)?

Haikang Vision, commonly known as Hikvision (full name: Hangzhou Hikvision Digital Technology Co., Ltd.), is a leading Chinese multinational corporation specializing in video surveillance equipment, security systems, and AIoT (AI + Internet of Things) solutions. Founded in 2001 and headquartered in Hangzhou, it's partly state-owned and one of the world's largest suppliers of CCTV cameras, network video recorders, and related technologies for both civilian and government applications.
