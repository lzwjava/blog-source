"Causal" is an adjective in English that relates to **cause and effect**.

Here are the main meanings:

* **Involving a cause; causing:** It describes something that acts as a cause or is an essential part of a cause.
    * *Example:* "The lightning strike was the **causal** agent of the fire."
* **Relating to causation or a link between cause and effect:** It describes a relationship where one thing makes another thing happen.
    * *Example:* "Researchers found a **causal** link between lack of sleep and poor concentration."
