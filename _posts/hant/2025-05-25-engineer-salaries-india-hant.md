### **影響薪資的關鍵因素**
1. **地點**：海得拉巴因聚集更多全球科技巨頭，薪資通常略高於浦那。兩城市因生活成本較高和人才需求旺盛，薪資均高於二線城市。[](https://www.weekday.works/post/average-salary-software-engineer-india)
2. **公司類型**：產品型企業（如Google、微軟）薪資比服務型企業（如TCS、Infosys）高20-30%。初創公司可能以股權補償較低的基本薪資。[](https://www.weekday.works/post/average-salary-software-engineer-india)[](https://www.crampete.com/blogs/software-developer-salary-in-india/)
3. **專業領域**：AI、機器學習、雲端運算（AWS、Azure）或DevOps等技能，可使各經驗層級薪資增加10-20%。[](https://www.weekday.works/post/average-salary-software-engineer-india)[](https://www.upgrad.com/blog/software-engineer-developer-salary-in-india-freshers-experienced/)
4. **教育背景**：IIT、NIT等頂尖學府畢業生通常起薪較高（多出10-20萬盧比）。[](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/software-engineer-salary/)
5. **額外薪酬**：獎金、股票期權和福利每年可增加5-50萬盧比，尤其在跨國企業。[](https://www.levels.fyi/t/software-engineer/locations/india)
