### **Ingredient Substitutions & Tips:**  
#### **Milk Options:**  
- **Best:** Whole milk (creamy) or evaporated milk (richer).  
- **Lighter:** Low-fat milk (add 1 extra yolk for richness).  
- **Dairy-free:** Oat milk + 1 tsp cornstarch (less creamy).  

#### **Sugar Options:**  
- Granulated sugar works best (dissolves evenly).  
- For caramel notes, use **brown sugar** (reduce by 1 tbsp).  
- Sugar-free? Substitute with **erythritol** (1:1 ratio).  

#### **Heavy Cream Alternatives:**  
- **Simpler:** Use 50% milk + 50% evaporated milk.  
- **Luxury:** Double cream (extra rich, less stable).  
- **Dairy-free:** Coconut cream (shaken, not stirred).  

#### **Pastry Shortcuts:**  
- **No shells?** Use puff pastry cut into circles.  
- **Extra flaky?** Brush shells with melted butter pre-baking.
