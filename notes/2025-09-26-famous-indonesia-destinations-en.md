Here are some of the most famous places in Indonesia:

### 1. **Bali**
   - Known for its stunning beaches, tropical rainforests, temples, and unique culture.
   - Popular attractions: Ubud, Kuta, Uluwatu, and Tirta Empul Temple.

### 2. **Jakarta**
   - The capital city of Indonesia, a bustling metropolis with modern skyscrapers, museums, and historical sites.
   - Popular attractions: National Monument (Monas), Kota Tua (Old Town), and the Thousand Islands (Kepulauan Seribu).

### 3. **Yogyakarta**
   - The cultural heart of Indonesia, famous for its ancient temples and traditional arts.
   - Popular attractions: Borobudur Temple, Prambanan Temple, and the Kraton (Sultan's Palace).

### 4. **Bandung**
   - The capital of West Java, known for its cool climate, culinary delights, and creative culture.
   - Popular attractions: Tangkuban Perahu Volcano, Ciwidey Tea Plantations, and factory outlet shopping.

### 5. **Lombok**
   - A peaceful island east of Bali, renowned for its pristine beaches and surfing spots.
   - Popular attractions: Gili Islands and Mount Rinjani.

### 6. **Sulawesi**
   - Famous for its unique shape and rich marine biodiversity.
   - Popular attractions: Tana Toraja traditional villages and Bunaken Island for diving.

### 7. **Sumatra**
   - One of Indonesia's largest islands, home to dense rainforests and diverse wildlife.
   - Popular attractions: Lake Toba and Bukit Lawang in Gunung Leuser National Park.

### 8. **Papua**
   - Indonesia's easternmost province, known for its untouched natural beauty and distinct cultures.
