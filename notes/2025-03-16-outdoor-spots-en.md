#### Detailed Findings

##### Baiyun Mountain Park
- **Location and Overview**: Located in Baiyun District, Baiyun Mountain Park spans 28 square kilometers and is a national 5A-level scenic area, known as the "lung of Guangzhou" due to its forest coverage ([China Highlights - Guangzhou Baiyun Mountain](https://www.chinahighlights.com/guangzhou/attraction/white-clouds-mountain.htm)).
- **Activities**: Offers hiking trails of varying difficulty, cable car rides, and walking paths. Popular for exercise, with options to reach Moxing Summit via shuttle or on foot ([TripAdvisor - Baiyun Mountain](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g298555-d490438-Reviews-Baiyun_Mountain-Guangzhou_Guangdong.html)).
- **Features**: Includes seven scenic areas, such as Luhu Lake for lakeside walks and Yuntai Garden for rare plants. Provides panoramic city views, making it ideal for scenic outdoor sports.
- **Suitability**: Fits the criteria with its mountain setting, lake views, and good park facilities, aligning with your interest in natural settings with views.

##### Panyu Xinghai Park
- **Location and Overview**: Situated in Panyu District, Panyu Xinghai Park is a large urban park known for its recreational offerings ([TripAdvisor - Panyu Xinghai Park](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g298555-d1797582-Reviews-Panyu_Xinghai_Park-Guangzhou_Guangdong.html)).
- **Activities**: Supports boating with paddle boats, walking, and cycling, with bike rentals available. Evening activities include dancing and martial arts, enhancing its appeal for outdoor sports.
- **Features**: Features a large pond crisscrossed by bridges, gardens, and picnic areas. Entrance is free, with amusements costing extra, making it accessible for various budgets.
- **Suitability**: Meets the criteria with its lake, good park facilities, and recreational activities, suitable for family-oriented outdoor sports.

##### Haizhu National Wetland Park
- **Location and Overview**: Located in Haizhu District, Haizhu National Wetland Park includes the Haizhu National Wetland Park, known as the "green heart" of Guangzhou ([TripAdvisor - Haizu Wetland](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g298555-d11816459-Reviews-Haizu_Wetland-Guangzhou_Guangdong.html)).
- **Activities**: Offers walking trails, birdwatching, and wetland exploration. Suitable for leisurely outdoor activities in a natural setting.
- **Features**: Spans 1,100 hectares with greenery, flowers, and clear lakes, providing a natural escape within the city. Ideal for those seeking tranquility and nature-based sports.
- **Suitability**: Aligns with the request for lakes and good parks, offering scenic views and outdoor activities like walking and observing wildlife.

##### Liwan Lake Park
- **Location and Overview**: Found in Liwan District, Liwan Lake Park is part of Lychee Bay, known for its historical and natural charm ([TripAdvisor - Liwan Lake Park](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g298555-d1805708-Reviews-Liwan_Lake_Park-Guangzhou_Guangdong.html)).
- **Activities**: Includes boating with pedal or electric boats, walking around multiple lakes (Xiaocui, Yucui, Ruyi, Wuxiu), and enjoying pavilions. Suitable for family outings and leisurely sports.
- **Features**: Covers 27 hectares, with 62% water area, featuring ancient houses and gardens. Offers a serene environment for outdoor recreation, with boat rentals at 100 RMB for 30 minutes.
- **Suitability**: Fits the criteria with its lakes, good park facilities, and historical views, ideal for boating and walking.

##### Helong Reservoir Scenic Area
- **Location and Overview**: Located in Baiyun District, Helong Reservoir was upgraded to a secondary protection zone for drinking water sources in 2016, now serving as an eco-heaven ([Helong Reservoir thrives as eco-heaven in Guangdong](https://en.by.gov.cn/2024-07/05/c_1020391.htm)).
