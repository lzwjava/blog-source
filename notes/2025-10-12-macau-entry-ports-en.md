Going to Macau, the main ports you can choose include the following, depending on where you depart from and the transportation method:

1. **Zhuhai Direction**:
   - **Gongbei Port**: The busiest land port, connecting Zhuhai to Macau's main urban area, suitable for walking or taking a vehicle across the border, open 24 hours.
   - **Hengqin Port**: Mainly serves the Hengqin New Area and Macau exchanges, suitable for self-driving or taking a vehicle, open 24 hours.
   - **Qingmao Port**: A newer port, connecting Zhuhai Qingmao to Macau, suitable for walking across the border, open from 7:00-22:00.
   - **Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macau Bridge Port** (Zhuhai Highway Port): Connected via the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macau Bridge to Hong Kong, Zhuhai, and Macau, suitable for cross-border buses or self-driving, open 24 hours.

2. **Hong Kong Direction**:
   - **Macau Outer Harbour Ferry Terminal**: The main port for taking ferries from Hong Kong to Macau, located on the Macau Peninsula, operates 24 hours.
   - **Taipa Ferry Terminal**: Located in Taipa, Macau, suitable for arriving from Hong Kong or Shenzhen by ferry, operating hours usually 7:00 to next day 1:00.
   - **Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macau Bridge Macau Port**: Enter Macau from Hong Kong via the bridge, suitable for cross-border buses or self-driving, open 24 hours.

3. **Shenzhen Direction**:
   - **Shekou Port**: Take a ferry from Shenzhen Shekou Port to Macau, arriving at Macau's Outer Harbour Ferry Terminal or Taipa Ferry Terminal, voyage about 1 hour, operating hours usually 8:00-21:30.
   - **Shenzhen Airport Fuyong Port**: Take a ferry from Fuyong Port near Shenzhen Bao'an Airport to Macau, suitable for air + water combined transport, operating hours 8:30-20:30.

4. **Other Ports**:
   - **Macau International Airport**: If you choose to fly, Macau International Airport is the main air entry port, suitable for international or mainland cities (such as Shanghai, Beijing) passengers.
   - **Cross-border Industrial Zone Port**: Mainly for cargo and specific personnel exchanges, less used by ordinary travelers.
