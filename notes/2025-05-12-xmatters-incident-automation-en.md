## What Is xMatters?

**xMatters** is a **service reliability platform** designed for DevOps, SRE, IT Ops, and continuity teams. It enables automation of workflows, ensures infrastructure and applications stay up and running, and accelerates product delivery—all with the aim of improving customer satisfaction.([help.xmatters.com][1])

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Workflow Automation & Incident Response

* Built around a **no-code/low-code workflow builder (Flow Designer)**, xMatters lets you create and customize incident response workflows easily using templates or drag-and-drop components.([xMatters][2])
* Useful for reacting to deployments, infrastructure disruptions, or system alerts with automated actions and escalation paths.([xMatters][2])

### 2. Intelligent Alerting & Signal Management

* Filters out noise by correlating and suppressing redundant alerts, enabling focus on critical issues.([xMatters][3])
* Sends alerts to the **right people** based on skills, availability, on-call schedules, escalation policies, and device preferences.([xMatters][3])
* Enriches notifications with context from across systems—making it easier to infer the scope and severity of issues.([xMatters][4])

### 3. Analytics & Ongoing Improvement

* Features include detailed **post-incident reports**, "play-back" event timelines, and real-time service-level indicators.([xMatters][3])
* These insights help teams review performance, enhance accountability, and prevent recurring incidents.

### 4. Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure

* Globally available, secure cloud infrastructure compliant with ISO 27001 and NIST standards.([xMatters][3])
* Supports hybrid environments—cloud and on-prem—even behind firewalls.([xMatters][3])
* Offers **role-based access control** to align permissions with organizational needs.([xMatters][3])

### 5. Wide-Ranging Integrations

* Integrates with hundreds of tools across monitoring, ITSM, communication, and DevOps ecosystems—like Slack, Jira, ServiceNow, Dynatrace, Teams, New Relic, Azure, Google Cloud, and more.([xMatters][5], [Appsource – Business Apps][6], [help.xmatters.com][7])
* Especially notable is its integration with Dynatrace, where xMatters can act on automated root cause alerts to orchestrate remediation tasks.([Dynatrace][8])

### 6. Digital Operations Platform (Everbridge OEM)

* As part of the **Everbridge Digital Operations Platform**, xMatters extends its functionality to broader incident, risk, and event-response use cases—ideal for business continuity and resilience.([xMatters][9])
* Trusted by leading global organizations, with metrics like Kellogg’s reporting an **83% reduction in MTTR**, **92% lower resource costs**, and **99.99% alerting accuracy**.([xMatters][5])
