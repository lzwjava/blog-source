Quick tips & gotchas:

* Your mapping has **two `deepseek-v3` keys**; in Python dicts the **later one overwrites the earlier**. If you want both paid and `:free` routes, use distinct keys (e.g., `"deepseek-v3-free"` vs `"deepseek-v3"`).
