Lobachevsky State University of Nizhny Novgorod (UNN), officially known as National Research Lobachevsky State University of Nizhny Novgorod, is a prominent public research university located in the historic city of Nizhny Novgorod, Russia. Founded on January 31, 1916, it holds the distinction of being the first institution of higher education in the city and one of the earliest state universities in Soviet Russia. The university was named in honor of the eminent Russian mathematician Nikolai Lobachevsky, a native of Nizhny Novgorod, in 1956.

Recognized as a National Research University since 2009, UNN is among the leading classical universities in Russia. It is a large educational and scientific center, with a student body exceeding 30,000, including a growing number of international students from various countries. The university consistently ranks among the top universities in Russia and is featured in various international rankings, reflecting its commitment to academic excellence and research.

Lobachevsky University offers a wide array of programs across numerous faculties and institutes, covering a broad spectrum of disciplines in exact sciences, natural sciences, humanities, and social sciences. It has historically been at the forefront of developing new fields, establishing the first Faculty of Radiophysics in the USSR in 1945 and the first Faculty of Computational Mathematics and Cybernetics in 1963.

The university is particularly strong in research, with significant advancements in areas such as physics, engineering, chemistry, and increasingly, biomedicine. It houses several research institutes and world-class laboratories, including a Supercomputing Center. UNN is actively involved in interdisciplinary research, notably in its biomedical cluster, which focuses on cutting-edge research in fields like neurobiology, oncology, and genetic engineering.

UNN's infrastructure includes a sprawling campus in Nizhny Novgorod, well-equipped libraries with extensive collections, modern research facilities, and various cultural and sports amenities.
