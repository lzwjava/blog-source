### Overview
Microsoft Teams has introduced numerous new functionalities from July 2023 to July 2025, focusing on enhancing collaboration, AI integration, meetings, events, phone capabilities, devices, and security. These updates aim to improve productivity, inclusivity, and hybrid work experiences. Below, I've grouped key features by category, highlighting major additions with brief descriptions and approximate release timelines. Features are drawn from official announcements, prioritizing those rolled out or announced in the specified period.

### Chat and Collaboration
- **Custom Emojis and Reactions**: Users can upload custom emojis and reactions to personalize conversations and reflect team culture (July 2024).
- **Loop Components in Channels**: Co-create and sync content like tables, lists, and trackers directly in channels (December 2023).
- **Improved Search Experience**: Enhanced in-chat/channel search with "find in channel" button and domain-specific filters (e.g., file types or names) (December 2023).
- **Compact Chat List and Mark All as Read**: Condense chat views and clear notifications in one click (June 2023).
- **Message Forwarding**: Forward messages (including Loop components and app cards) between chats and channels, with added context (January 2025).
- **Copilot Enhancements**: Time-based prompts (e.g., "last month") for chat history searches (August 2024); file summaries in chats for quick insights without opening documents (early 2025).
- **Slash Commands**: Quick actions like opening chats in new windows or adding code blocks via "/" in compose box (May 2024).
- **Code Blocks in Loop**: Share and co-edit code snippets in chats/channels (June 2024).
- **Channel Cards**: Hover over channel names for snapshots of descriptions, members, and settings (August 2024).
- **New Chat and Channels Experience**: Unified view for chats/channels, custom sections, filters; rolled out to GCC (June 2025).
- **Group Chat Profile Pictures**: Custom images or emojis for group chats (April 2024).
- **@Nearby in Chat**: Connect with physically nearby colleagues (April 2025).
- **Storyline Integration**: Leaders share updates in Teams, simplifying employee communications (early 2025).

### Meetings, Webinars, and Town Halls
- **Spatial Audio**: Positions voices in gallery view for immersive audio (June 2023).
- **Avatars and Reactions**: Custom avatars with new reactions and personal boundaries in immersive spaces (July 2024).
- **Collaborative Notes**: Real-time co-editing of agendas/notes/tasks via Loop, syncing with To Do/Planner (October 2023); now in education and GCC-High/DoD (June 2025).
- **Intelligent Meeting Recap**: AI-generated summaries, notes, tasks; extended to calls/Meet Now (December 2024); multilingual support (early 2025).
- **Town Hall Features**: New template for large events (up to 20,000 attendees), green room, RTMP-in, live reactions, Q&A export (October 2023); DVR (pause/rewind) for streaming (March 2025); eDiscovery support (January 2025).
- **Webinar Enhancements**: Custom registration, presenter bios/theming, attendee reporting (January 2023); now in GCC (September 2024).
- **Copilot in Meetings**: Sources from transcript and chat for responses; multi-turn conversations (April 2024); controls to disable (September 2024).
- **Breakout Rooms**: Participant shuffle, self-move between rooms (July 2023, January 2025).
- **Voice Isolation and Portrait Blur**: AI noise suppression and background effects (April 2024).
- **Mesh Events**: Templates (e.g., Meadow/Oasis), revisit links, multi-room reactions (July 2024); audio for screen sharing (January 2025).
- **Multilingual Transcription**: Supports 51 spoken/31 translation languages (early 2025).
- **Display Name Editing**: Change names in meetings (April 2025).

### Teams Phone and Calls
- **Copilot in Teams Phone**: AI for unscheduled calls, note-taking, real-time coaching (July 2023).
- **Teams Phone Mobile**: Expanded to Norway (December 2023).
- **Delayed Simultaneous Ring**: Configures primary recipient before notifying others (February 2025).
- **Direct Routing Management**: Upload/manage phone numbers in admin center (February 2025).
- **Queues App Enhancements**: Barge/whisper/monitor/takeover for supervisors (March 2025).
- **Extensibility for Contact Centers**: Integration with Dynamics 365 and ISVs via Azure (April 2025).
- **Shared Line Access**: Share phone lines with groups without delegation (early 2025).
- **Brand Impersonation Protection**: Detects phishing in external calls (November 2024).

### Workplace, Places, and Teams Rooms
- **Workplace Presence**: Auto-updates location to "office" via desk connections or Wi-Fi; see nearby colleagues (September 2024, early 2025).
- **Bookable Desks**: Auto-reserve via monitor connection; discoverability/analytics (June 2024, early 2024).
- **Ultrasound Proximity Join**: Detect nearby rooms for easy joining (September 2024).
- **Speaker Recognition**: Attributes voices in transcripts/recaps for all mics (July 2024).
- **Multi-Camera Views**: Up to four streams in rooms (January 2025).
- **AI Assistant in Pro Management**: Query for insights on rooms/management (June 2025).
- **Text/UI Resizing**: Adjust display scaling for better readability (August 2024).
- **Room as Presenter**: Join webinars/town halls from room console (February 2025).

### Devices and Hardware
- **New Certified Devices**: Regular additions like Lenovo ThinkSmart Core, Yealink MVC860, Poly G7500 (June 2023); Owl Bar, Yealink Room Panel E2 (February 2025, December 2024).
- **Device Store in Teams App**: Discover/purchase headsets, phones, Rooms within Teams (April 2024).
- **Auto Updates for Android Devices**: Seamless app updates (December 2023).
- **BYOD Enhancements**: QR reservations, shared display mode, intelligent speakers (November 2023).

### AI, Copilot, and Agents
- **Agents in Teams**: Add AI agents/bots to chats/meetings; mobile support (May 2025).
- **Copilot Compose**: Adjust draft messages with custom prompts (June 2024).
- **Agentic Memory and Protocols**: Agents recall interactions, communicate peer-to-peer (May 2025).
- **Copilot in Admin Center**: Insights on policies, troubleshooting (early 2025).
- **Space Analytics with Copilot**: Query room usage data (November 2024).

### Fundamentals, Security, and Admin
- **New Teams App**: Faster, lighter app with VDI/government support (October 2023).
- **VDI Optimization**: Enhanced performance, codecs, backgrounds (June 2024, April 2025).
- **Multi-Account Sign-In**: Across clouds without switching (April 2024).
- **Information Barriers in Meetings**: Hold meetings with conflicting policies via moderator (April 2025).
- **SMS Notifications for Bookings**: For all appointments (November 2024).
- **eCDN Enhancements**: Zero-config, GCC support (November 2024).

### Frontline Worker Solutions
- **Shifts App Updates**: Easier shift management, notifications (ongoing, e.g., May 2025 Build).
- **Queues App for Frontline**: Monitor/coach agents (March 2025).

These updates reflect Microsoft's emphasis on AI-driven productivity and hybrid work. For the full rollout status, check the Microsoft 365 Roadmap.
