The main cities in the Philippines include:

### 1. **Manila**
- The capital city.
- Located in the National Capital Region (NCR).
- Part of **Metro Manila**, the country’s political, economic, and cultural center.

### 2. **Quezon City**
- The most populous city in the Philippines.
- Also in **Metro Manila**.
- Home to government offices like the House of Representatives.

### 3. **Cebu City**
- Located in **Cebu province**, Visayas region.
- The "Queen City of the South."
- Major economic and cultural hub outside Luzon.

### 4. **Davao City**
- Located in **Mindanao**.
- Largest city in terms of land area.
- Known for cleanliness, order, and being the home of former President Rodrigo Duterte.

### 5. **Makati**
- Also in **Metro Manila**.
- Central business district (CBD) of the Philippines.
- Financial and commercial heart of the country.

### 6. **Taguig**
- Rapidly developing city in Metro Manila.
- Home to **Bonifacio Global City (BGC)**, a modern business and lifestyle district.

### 7. **Pasig**
- Also part of Metro Manila.
- Contains the Ortigas Center, another major business district.

### 8. **Cagayan de Oro**
- Located in Northern Mindanao.
- Known as the “City of Golden Friendship.”

### 9. **Zamboanga City**
- Located in the southwestern part of Mindanao.
- Known for its unique culture and the Chavacano language.

### 10. **Iloilo City**
- Located in Western Visayas.
- Historic and cultural center with Spanish-era architecture.
