Okay, let's plan an exciting 20-day USA adventure! To give you the best plan, I'll assume you're interested in seeing a mix of iconic landmarks, diverse landscapes, and vibrant cities. This itinerary will cover both coasts and some highlights in between.

**Please note:** This is a packed itinerary. You can adjust it based on your interests and preferred pace. Domestic flights are assumed for longer distances.

**Option 1: Coast-to-Coast Highlights (Mix of City & Nature)**

**Days 1-3: New York City, New York (East Coast)**

* **Day 1:** Arrive in New York City (JFK or Newark Airport). Check into your hotel. Explore Times Square, catch a Broadway show (book in advance!).
* **Day 2:** Visit the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island (book ferry tickets in advance). Explore the Financial District, see the Charging Bull and One World Observatory. Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge for stunning city views.
* **Day 3:** Immerse yourself in art at the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA). Stroll through Central Park. Explore diverse neighborhoods like Greenwich Village or SoHo.

**Days 4-5: Washington D.C. (East Coast)**

* **Day 4:** Take an early morning train (Amtrak) or a short flight to Washington D.C. Check into your hotel. Visit the White House and the National Mall, see the Lincoln Memorial and the Washington Monument.
* **Day 5:** Explore the Smithsonian National Museums (National Air and Space Museum, National Museum of Natural History, etc.). Visit the US Capitol Building and the Library of Congress (book tours in advance if possible).

**Day 6: Travel Day - Fly to Chicago (Midwest)**

* **Day 6:** Enjoy a final morning in D.C. Fly from Washington D.C. (DCA, IAD, or BWI) to Chicago (ORD or MDW). Check into your hotel. Enjoy a deep-dish pizza for dinner.

**Days 7-8: Chicago, Illinois (Midwest)**

* **Day 7:** Explore Millennium Park (home to "The Bean"). Take an architectural boat tour on the Chicago River. Visit the Art Institute of Chicago.
* **Day 8:** Go up the Willis Tower (formerly Sears Tower) or 360 Chicago for panoramic city views. Explore Navy Pier. Catch a blues or jazz performance.

**Day 9: Travel Day - Fly to Denver (Rocky Mountains)**

* **Day 9:** Enjoy a final morning in Chicago. Fly from Chicago (ORD or MDW) to Denver, Colorado (DEN). Check into your hotel.

**Days 10-11: Denver & Rocky Mountain National Park (Mountains & Nature)**

* **Day 10:** Explore downtown Denver, including Larimer Square and the 16th Street Mall. Visit the Denver Art Museum or the Denver Museum of Nature & Science.
* **Day 11:** Rent a car and take a day trip to Rocky Mountain National Park (about 1.5-hour drive). Enjoy scenic drives, hiking trails (depending on the season and your fitness level), and breathtaking mountain views.

**Day 12: Travel Day - Fly to Las Vegas (Southwest)**

* **Day 12:** Enjoy a final morning in Denver. Fly from Denver (DEN) to Las Vegas, Nevada (LAS). Check into your hotel on the Las Vegas Strip.

**Days 13-14: Las Vegas & Grand Canyon (Desert & Natural Wonder)**

* **Day 13:** Explore the famous Las Vegas Strip, see the Bellagio fountains, visit themed hotels like the Venetian or Caesars Palace. Catch a world-class show.
* **Day 14:** Take a day trip to the Grand Canyon National Park (South Rim is most popular). You can drive (approx. 4-5 hours each way) or take a guided tour (including bus or small plane options). Witness the awe-inspiring vastness of the canyon.

**Day 15: Travel Day - Fly to Los Angeles (West Coast)**

* **Day 15:** Enjoy a final morning in Las Vegas. Fly from Las Vegas (LAS) to Los Angeles, California (LAX). Check into your hotel.

**Days 16-17: Los Angeles, California (West Coast)**

* **Day 16:** Explore Hollywood: Walk the Hollywood Walk of Fame, see TCL Chinese Theatre, and try to spot the Hollywood Sign. Visit a movie studio like Universal Studios or Warner Bros. Studio Tour (book in advance).
* **Day 17:** Relax on the beaches of Santa Monica or Venice Beach. Visit the Getty Center for art and architecture with stunning views. Explore Beverly Hills and Rodeo Drive.

**Days 18-19: California Coast (Scenic Drive)**

* **Day 18:** Rent a car and drive south along the scenic Pacific Coast Highway (Highway 1) towards San Diego (approx. 2-3 hour drive without stops). Enjoy breathtaking ocean views. Stop at charming towns like Laguna Beach or Dana Point. Check into your hotel in San Diego.
* **Day 19:** Explore San Diego: Visit Balboa Park (home to numerous museums, gardens, and the San Diego Zoo). Explore the historic Gaslamp Quarter. Relax on Coronado Beach.

**Day 20: Departure from San Diego or Los Angeles**

* **Day 20:** Enjoy a final breakfast in the USA. Depending on your international flight, you can depart from San Diego International Airport (SAN) or drive back to Los Angeles (approx. 2-hour drive) to depart from LAX.
