### Information on 610201 and 11325 in Guangdong University of Foreign Studies Self-Study Exam

#### Overview
- **610201**: This is the professional code for the **Computer Application Technology (Associate Degree)** program (计算机应用技术专科) in Guangdong's self-study exam (自考) system. It falls under the "Computer and Applications" category. Guangdong University of Foreign Studies (GDUFS) serves as one of the main examining institutions for this major, alongside Guangdong University of Finance and Economics.
  - **Key Details**:
    - Level: Associate (专科).
    - Focus: Trains students for roles in computer technology application within enterprises and institutions, covering programming, networks, data structures, and more.
    - Core Courses (examples): Data Structure Introduction (02142), Advanced Language Programming (I) (00342), Computer Network Technology (02141), English (I) (00012), and practical components.
    - Updated Code: Note that the code has transitioned to 510201 in some recent updates, but 610201 is still referenced in 2024-2025 materials.

- **11325**: This is the course code for **Computer and Applications Course Experiment (I)** (计算机及应用课程实验(一)), a practical assessment course within the 610201 major (and related computer programs).
  - **Key Details**:
    - Type: Practical exam (实践考核), required after passing all theoretical (pen trial) courses in the major.
    - Purpose: Hands-on evaluation of computer application skills, such as programming experiments and system operations.
    - Requirements: Candidates must upload proof of passed theoretical scores and complete registration via the specified system. It's typically the final step before graduation eligibility.
    - Exam Outline: Available on the GDUFS Continuing Education website under "Self-Exam Services."

#### Registration Process for Practical Assessments (e.g., 11325 in 610201)
The attached PDF ("附件1：广东外语外贸大学实践考核报考操作流程（202506）.pdf") outlines the step-by-step process for social candidates (社会考生) registering for practical exams like 11325. This is for the June 2025 session (202506). Key steps:

1. **Preparation and Notes**:
   - Read the guide carefully. Provide accurate personal and course info (e.g., major code 610201, course code 11325). Errors or over-registrations are non-refundable; dishonesty leads to consequences.
   - For Hong Kong/Macau/Taiwan candidates: Email details (name, ID, origin, admission ticket number, major/course codes, phone, email) to gwdzkb@163.com with subject "港澳台考生实践考核信息" during the registration window. Late emails are not accepted.

2. **Login and Registration**:
   - Access: http://service.qidaedu.com/practiceLogin.jsp.
   - Register a new account if needed (fill in username, password, etc.).

3. **Enter Exam Scores**:
   - After login, click "笔试成绩" (Pen Trial Scores).
   - Upload "考生信息简表" (Candidate Info Summary Table) as a PDF (download from Guangdong Self-Exam Management System).
   - Add passed courses: Input details for theoretical courses in 610201 (e.g., scores for 02142, 00342) accurately.

4. **Declare Practical Courses**:
   - Click "实践申报" (Practical Declaration).
   - Select and apply for courses like 11325.
   - Submit and wait for review by the self-exam office. Results announced uniformly on June 18, 2025 (afternoon 3 PM).

5. **Payment**:
   - Only if status is "审核通过" (Review Passed). Delete any failed reviews to generate order.
   - Payment window: June 19, 2025 (10:00 AM) to June 23, 2025 (10:00 AM). Late payment = forfeiture.
   - Click "去缴费" (Go to Payment), verify amount matches practical fee, and complete via selected method (e.g., online bank).

6. **Post-Registration**:
   - Monitor email and phone for updates or issues. The office contacts via email/phone if anomalies occur.

For full exam outlines and updates, check the GDUFS self-exam portal. Ensure all theoretical courses in 610201 are passed before applying for 11325.
