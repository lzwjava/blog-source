### Hikvision's Major Products

Hikvision, a global leader in AIoT and video surveillance, offers a wide range of security and monitoring solutions. Their major product categories include:

- **Network Cameras and IP Products**: High-resolution IP cameras such as dome, bullet, mini-dome, box, PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom), panoramic, and specialized cameras for applications like thermal imaging or onboard vehicle security. These integrate AI-enhanced analytics for intelligent monitoring.
  
- **Recording and Storage Devices**: Network Video Recorders (NVRs), Digital Video Recorders (DVRs), and Turbo HD products for reliable video capture and storage.

- **Access Control Systems**: Comprehensive solutions including controllers, readers, and barrier gates for secure entry management.

- **Video Intercom and Communication**: Door stations, indoor monitors, and integrated systems for residential and commercial intercom setups.

- **Display and Control Solutions**: Video management software (VMS) like HikCentral, displays, and control panels for centralized monitoring and automation.

- **Transmission and Networking**: PoE switches, fiber optic transmission devices, and network infrastructure to support large-scale deployments.

- **Analytics and AIoT Software**: Advanced tools like Hik-Connect for remote access, intelligent search features, and AI-driven analytics for behavior detection and data processing.

These products cater to industries like retail, transportation, public safety, and smart cities, emphasizing AI integration for enhanced efficiency.
