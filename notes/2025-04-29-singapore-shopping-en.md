## Popular Shopping Malls

1. **ION Orchard** - A luxury mall on Orchard Road with designer brands, specialty stores and an art gallery on the top floor with panoramic city views.

2. **VivoCity** - Singapore's largest mall located at HarbourFront, featuring a rooftop amphitheater, playground, and direct access to Sentosa Island.

3. **Jewel Changi Airport** - Not just an airport mall but a destination with the world's tallest indoor waterfall (Rain Vortex) and over 280 shops and restaurants.

4. **Funan** - A tech-focused lifestyle mall with indoor cycling paths, rock climbing facilities and urban farm alongside electronics and lifestyle stores.

5. **Bugis Junction** - Connected to Bugis MRT, featuring a glass-covered shopping street experience with local and international brands.

## Famous Food Streets

1. **Chinatown Food Street** - Offering hawker stalls under a glass canopy serving local favorites like chili crab, satay, and Hainanese chicken rice.

2. **Lau Pa Sat** - Historic market with Victorian architecture that transforms into a bustling satay street in the evenings.

3. **Telok Ayer Street** - Mix of traditional hawker fare and trendy cafes in restored shophouses.

4. **East Coast Lagoon Food Village** - Seaside hawker center famous for seafood and local delights with ocean views.

5. **Tiong Bahru Market** - One of the oldest hawker centers with authentic local breakfast options and famous chwee kueh (steamed rice cakes).

## Business/Shopping Streets

1. **Orchard Road** - Singapore's premier shopping boulevard with dozens of malls, department stores, and flagship boutiques.

2. **Haji Lane** - Narrow street in Kampong Glam filled with independent boutiques, vintage shops, and Middle Eastern cafes.

3. **Arab Street** - Known for textile shops, Persian carpet stores, and Middle Eastern restaurants in the historic Kampong Glam district.

4. **Boat Quay/Clarke Quay** - Riverside commercial areas with shops, restaurants, and nightlife venues in restored shophouses.

5. **Tanjong Pagar** - Business district that transforms after office hours with Korean restaurants, boutique shops, and bars.
