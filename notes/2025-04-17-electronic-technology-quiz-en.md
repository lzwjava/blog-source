**Part I: Analog Electronics**

**Fundamentals of Circuit Analysis**

1. What is Ohm’s Law, and how is it mathematically expressed?
2. State Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL) and Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law (KVL).
3. If 3 amps flow through a 1-ohm resistor, what is the voltage across it?[^3]
4. What is the total resistance when three resistors of 1 Ω, 2 Ω, and 3 Ω are connected in series?[^3]
5. Explain the difference between nodal analysis and mesh analysis.

**Principles of Amplifier Circuits**

6. Name two types of semiconductor devices commonly used in amplifier circuits.
7. List the three basic amplifier configurations for BJTs and briefly describe each.
8. What is meant by the frequency response of an amplifier?
9. How does negative feedback affect the stability of an amplifier?

**Operational Amplifiers (Op-Amps) and Applications**

10. How many input terminals does a standard operational amplifier have?[^4]
11. What is meant by the “virtual short” concept in op-amp circuits?
12. Name two typical op-amp circuits and their basic functions.
13. Which op-amp application can be used to generate square or triangular waveforms?
14. What is the standard form of CMRR?[^4]

**DC Power Supplies**

15. What is the primary function of a rectifier circuit?
16. Compare linear regulators and switching regulators in terms of efficiency and complexity.

**Part II: Digital Electronics**

**Fundamentals of Digital Circuits**

17. In digital electronics, what binary value typically represents the ‘ON’ state?[^2]
18. Convert the decimal number 13 to binary.
19. What is the Boolean expression for the output of an AND gate with inputs A and B?[^2]
20. What is the difference between TTL and CMOS logic families?

**Combinational Logic Circuits**

21. What is a multiplexer, and what is its primary function?[^5]
22. Give an example of a combinational logic circuit and its application.
23. What is a hazard in combinational logic circuits, and how can it be eliminated?[^5]

**Sequential Logic Circuits**

24. Name four types of flip-flops and briefly describe one application for any type.
25. What is the main difference between synchronous and asynchronous sequential circuits?
26. How many flip-flops are required to design a counter with 16 states?[^6]
27. What is the function of a shift register?

**Programmable Logic Devices (PLDs)**

28. Name three types of PLDs and give one application for each.
29. What is HDL, and why is it important in digital system design?
30. Write a simple VHDL or Verilog statement to define a 2-input AND gate.
