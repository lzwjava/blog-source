### Latest Windows 11 Update
Windows 11's most recent major feature update is **version 24H2 (Sun Valley 3)**, also known as the October 2024 Update. It includes improvements like enhanced task management, a redesigned File Explorer, better gaming features (e.g., optimizations for GPU scheduling and games like Starfield), integrated Copilot+ PC capabilities, predictive polling for fluent transitions, and bug fixes for stability and security.

This update rolled out gradually:
- Preview builds started in May 2024 via Windows Insider.
- The enablement package became available on September 3, 2024, for eligible devices (though some features, like Recall AI, faced delays and are still rolling out).
- The full public release completed worldwide by October 1, 2024.

To check your device, go to Settings > Windows Update and look for "2024-10 Cumulative Update" or newer.
