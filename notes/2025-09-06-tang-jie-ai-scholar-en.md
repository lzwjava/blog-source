## Who is Tang Jie?

### 1. **Academic Background**

* Tang Jie is a **WeBank Chair Professor** at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, **Tsinghua University**, where he also completed his **PhD in 2006**. He’s notably recognized in fields including AI, social networks, data mining, machine learning, and knowledge graphs ([Wikipedia][1], [cs.tsinghua.edu.cn][2]).
* He’s been honored as a **Fellow of the IEEE, ACM, and AAAI**—achievements spanning from 2021 through 2023 ([Wikipedia][1], [iswc2023.semanticweb.org][3]).

### 2. **Academic Achievements**

* He is the inventor of **AMiner (ArnetMiner)**, an academic social network search platform that has drawn tens of millions of users from over 200 countries ([Knowledge Engineering Group][4], [iswc2023.semanticweb.org][3], [Wikipedia][1]).
* Award recognitions include:

  * **SIGKDD Test-of-Time Award**
  * **NSFC Distinguished Young Scholar**
  * **Second National Award for Science & Technology**
  * **SIGKDD Service Award** ([Knowledge Engineering Group][4], [iswc2023.semanticweb.org][3]).
* In 2025, he was recognized by **Research.com** as a **Computer Science in China Leader**, a title he has held since at least 2023 ([Research.com][5]).

### 3. **Z.ai / Zhipu AI Co-Founder**

* Tang Jie co-founded **Beijing Zhipu Huazhang Technology Co., Ltd.**, known publicly as **Zhipu AI** (and rebranded in 2025 as **Z.ai**), in **2019**. He founded it alongside **Li Juanzi**, another Tsinghua professor ([Wikipedia][6], [Rockrose][7]).
* Z.ai is considered one of China’s leading AI startups—sometimes referred to as an "AI Tiger"—and ranks among the top three large language model (LLM) market players in China according to IDC ([Wikipedia][6]).
* The company spun out of Tsinghua University, with backing from major investors including Alibaba, Tencent, Meituan, Xiaomi, and Saudi Arabia’s Prosperity7 Ventures ([Wikipedia][6], [Rockrose][7]).
* Z.ai has developed notable models and capabilities, such as:

  * **ChatGLM**: A series of pretrained dialogue models developed in collaboration with Tsinghua KEG ([Wikipedia][6]).
  * Other advanced AI offerings: **GLM-4**, **GLM-4.5**, **GLM-4.5V** (a vision-language model), **Ying** (a text-to-video model), and **AutoGLM** (a voice-driven AI agent app) ([Wikipedia][6]).
