To apply for a Singapore travel visa as a Chinese citizen in Guangzhou, follow these organized steps:

### 1. **Authorized Visa Agent Submission**
   - Singapore visas for Chinese citizens must be processed through **authorized agents** (individual applications to the embassy are not accepted).
   - **Find an Agent**: Check the Singapore Immigration & Checkpoints Authority (ICA) website or contact the Singapore Embassy in China for a list of authorized agents in Guangzhou. Examples include travel agencies like Ctrip (携程) or China Travel Service (中旅), but verify their current authorization status.

### 2. **Required Documents**
   - **Passport**: Original + copy (valid ≥6 months, with ≥2 blank pages).
   - **Visa Application Form**: Completed Form 14A (download from ICA website) with recent passport-sized photo (35mm x 45mm, white background).
   - **Proof of Employment/Studies**:
     - Employed: Company letter (position, salary, leave approval).
     - Student: Enrollment proof + school leave letter.
     - Self-employed/Freelancer: Business license or financial statements.
   - **Financial Proof**: Bank statements (last 3-6 months).
   - **Travel Itinerary**: Flight bookings (return ticket) + hotel reservation.
   - **Additional Documents**:
     - Invitation letter (if visiting family/friends) with inviter’s Singapore ID/passport copy.
     - For minors: Birth certificate + parental consent letter.

### 3. **Submission Process**
   - Submit documents to the authorized agent **in person** or **online** (if supported by the agency).
   - **Processing Time**: Typically 3-5 working days (apply at least 2 weeks before travel).

### 4. **Fees**
   - Visa fee: SGD 30 (~¥150) + agent service fee (varies; confirm with the agency).

### 5. **Receive e-Visa**
   - Once approved, the e-visa will be emailed. **Print a copy** to present during travel.

### 6. **Key Notes**
   - **Validity**: Usually 30 days (single/multiple entry; duration determined by ICA).
   - **No Visa-Free Entry**: Chinese citizens must obtain a visa before travel.
   - **Check Details**: Ensure all information matches your passport to avoid entry issues.

### 7. **Post-Approval**
   - Track application via the agent’s portal. For urgent queries, contact the agent directly.

### Resources
   - Singapore ICA: [https://www.ica.gov.sg](https://www.ica.gov.sg)
   - Singapore Embassy in China: Contact via website for agent lists.

Apply early, double-check documents, and confirm agent legitimacy through official channels. Safe travels!
