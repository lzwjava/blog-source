- Use the Shortcuts app to create a shortcut that triggers when you arrive at the office. This shortcut will automatically open the WeCom app, enabling you to punch in.

- When using a Macao SIM card as a cellular network, you don’t need a VPN to access global networks. However, it becomes inconvenient to switch to a VPN when connecting to Wi-Fi. Shortcuts can help with this.

- You can use shortcuts to notify you to play basketball or do other activities.

- Shortcuts are great for notifications that are helpful to have but not essential if they’re missing.

- Use shortcuts to remind you to pay your mortgage, monthly credit bills, or register for exams.
