## PayPal CN

- Website: [https://www.paypal.cn](https://www.paypal.cn)
- Required to upload a national identity card
- Face scanning is required
- Need to provide a business URL (e.g., shop or social media)
- Must input a mobile phone number

## Stripe

- Website: [https://stripe.com](https://stripe.com)
- China is not available in the list of supported countries
- For Singapore registration, a FIN (Foreign Identification Number) is required
- Therefore, Chinese citizens who lack the required information are not allowed to use it.

### Databricks

- Website: [http://databricks.com](http://databricks.com)
- Professional mode requires linking to a cloud provider (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud)
- Creates a stack in your AWS environment
- Once AWS stack creation is complete, it becomes accessible in the Databricks workspace
- Supports payment with China-issued Visa cards
- Using the product requires creating resources in AWS, incurring an estimated cost of 15 USD per week. Please be aware of this potential expense.
