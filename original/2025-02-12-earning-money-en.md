## Earning Money

- Worked as a software engineer for companies.

- Received stock options from a startup and earned a small amount of money after the startup was sold.

- Created a startup for a knowledge live broadcast platform and earned revenue from live attendance fees.

- Developed a script to help with course registration and sold it to schoolmates.

- Regularly invested in stocks or Bitcoin for the long term.

- Discovered a cryptocurrency wallet with tens of CNY in it, which grew to hundreds of CNY over the years.

- Helped a friend recruit someone and received a small referral bonus.

- Completed software projects for clients.

- Made a short trade with Trump coins and earned 600 USD dollars.

- Provided software-related assistance to friends and received red envelopes as payment.

## Losing Money

This post documents instances of financial losses experienced by myself and people around me.

- Entering a new industry or starting a business without a long-term plan and buying many ads will result in losses of several million CNY.

- Received an investment, quickly hired employees, and then had to lay them off after a few months. This resulted in a loss of 250,000 CNY.

- Buying a house in China around 2022 would have led to a loss of 1 million CNY.

- Buying a lot of ads to advertise an app will lead to a loss of half a million CNY.

- Buying a car or expensive things to impress others.

- Frequent trading of stocks or options within a short timeframe.

- Insufficient preparation for a new job or opportunity can result in job loss.
